# Review: Decide & Sign-off

## Need
- All findings discussed and resolved
- Review Facilitator ready to decide outcome

## Instructions

### 1. Determine Outcome
| Outcome | Condition | Action |
|---------|-----------|--------|
| ✅ Approved | No blockers, no more than N majors (per team policy) | Proceed to next step |
| 🔄 Changes Required | Blockers or significant majors identified | Author addresses, re-review only changed parts |
| ❌ Rejected | Fundamental flaw, wrong approach, or too many blockers | Restart or escalate to design discussion |

### 2. Record Sign-off
1. Each reviewer records their sign-off: approve, approve with changes, or reject
2. Review Facilitator confirms the outcome

### 3. Document Review Record
1. Artifact reviewed
2. Reviewers and their roles
3. All findings with classifications and resolutions
4. Outcome and sign-off
5. Any re-review needed (scope and timeline)

## Done
- Outcome determined
- Sign-off recorded from all reviewers
- Review record documented
