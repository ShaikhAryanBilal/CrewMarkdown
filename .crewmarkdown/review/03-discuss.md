# Review: Discuss

## Need
- Reviewers have individual findings
- Review Facilitator runs the discussion

## Instructions

### 1. Walk Through Findings
1. Each reviewer presents their findings (one at a time)
2. Author responds to questions and clarifies
3. Group discusses each finding:
   - Is it valid?
   - Can it be addressed simply?
   - Does it need broader design discussion?

### 2. Resolve or Escalate
1. For each blocking and major finding, decide:
   - **Accept** — finding is valid, author agrees to fix
   - **Reject** — finding is not valid, rationale documented
   - **Defer** — valid but out of scope, moved to separate ticket
   - **Escalate** — needs broader discussion → escalate to Debate or Meeting mode

### 3. Summarize
1. List all findings with their resolution
2. Count: blockers, majors, suggestions, questions

## Done
- All findings discussed and resolved (accept/reject/defer/escalate)
- Summary of findings documented
