# Review: Present

## Need
- Reviewers assembled
- Review packet distributed

## Instructions

### 1. Author Presents
1. Author walks through the artifact:
   - What was done and why
   - Key decisions made
   - Parts they want special attention on
   - Any concerns or trade-offs they're aware of

### 2. Reviewers Read/Explore
1. Reviewers take time to read/explore the artifact individually
2. Take notes on findings, questions, suggestions, and issues

### 3. Classification Scheme
Each finding should be classified:
| Label | Meaning | Action |
|-------|---------|--------|
| 🚫 Blocking | Must fix before ship | Blocks sign-off |
| ⚠️ Major | Should fix, significant concern | Address this sprint |
| 💡 Suggestion | Nice to have, improvement | Consider for backlog |
| ❓ Question | Clarification needed | Author responds |
| ✅ Praise | This is well done | Keep doing this |

## Done
- Author presented the artifact
- Reviewers read and noted their findings
- Findings classified by severity
