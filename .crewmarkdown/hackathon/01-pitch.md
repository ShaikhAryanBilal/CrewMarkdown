# Hackathon: Pitch & Form Teams

## Need
- Hackathon event planned
- Theme or problem space identified

## Instructions

### 1. Define Theme
1. What's the hackathon theme? (e.g., "AI-Powered Developer Tools", "Customer Experience")
2. What problems are in scope?
3. What's out of scope?
4. What resources are available? (APIs, datasets, cloud credits, mentors)

### 2. Pitch Session
1. Anyone can pitch an idea (2 min per pitch)
2. Each pitch should cover:
   - Problem being solved
   - Proposed solution (brief)
   - Skills needed on the team
   - A "sponsor" or champion for the idea
3. Collect all pitches in a shared document

### 3. Team Formation
1. Participants vote on which pitches interest them
2. Form teams of 3-5 people around pitches
3. Ensure each team has the skills they need
4. Teams pick a name

### 4. Finalize Team Goals
1. Each team defines their hackathon goal:
   - What will they build?
   - What's the "done" criteria for their demo?
   - What's the stretch goal if they finish early?
2. Teams commit to their goal

## Done
- Theme and scope defined
- Pitches presented and teams formed
- Each team has a name and clear goal
