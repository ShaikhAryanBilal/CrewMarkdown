# Retrospective: Gather Data

## Need
- Stage set, format chosen
- Team ready to share

## Instructions

### 1. Individual Reflection (5 min)
1. Each person silently writes their reflections
2. Use the chosen format's categories:
   - Start/Stop/Continue: what to start doing, stop doing, continue doing
   - Glad/Sad/Mad: what made you glad, sad, mad
   - Liked/Learned/Lacked/Longed For: per category
2. One insight per sticky note / card

### 2. Share (round-robin)
1. Each person shares their items one at a time
2. No debate or discussion yet — just present
3. Group similar items together

### 3. Group & Cluster
1. Facilitator groups related items into themes
2. Name each theme (e.g., "Communication", "Technical Debt", "Process")
3. Vote as a group on which themes to discuss

### 4. Dot Vote on Priorities
1. Each person gets N votes (usually 3-5)
2. Vote on the items/themes they want to discuss most
3. Top voted items become the discussion agenda

## Done
- All team members contributed their reflection
- Items grouped into themes
- Priority themes identified via dot voting
- Discussion agenda ready
