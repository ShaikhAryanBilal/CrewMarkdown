# Negotiation: Log

## Need
- Agreement reached or partial agreement with follow-up

## Instructions

### 1. Compile Negotiation Record
```markdown
# Negotiation Record: [topic]

- **Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
- **Mediator**: [role]
- **Parties**: [roles]
- **Issue**: [what needed deciding]

## Positions
| Party | Position | Underlying Interest |
|-------|----------|-------------------|
| A | ... | ... |
| B | ... | ... |

## Options Considered
1. [option] — [why it didn't work / why it was chosen]

## Agreement
[Full description of what was agreed]

## Terms
| Term | Detail | Owner | Deadline |
|------|--------|-------|----------|
| ... | ... | ... | ... |

## Unresolved Items (if any)
1. [item] — [follow-up plan]

## Sign-off
- Party A: [confirmed]
- Party B: [confirmed]
- Mediator: [recorded]
```

### 2. Archive
1. Log to `.crewmarkdown/logs/<yyyy>/<MM>/<dd>/<HHmmss>/<chat-slug>-[<HHmmss>]/negotiation-record.md`

### 3. Distribute
1. Share agreement with all parties and relevant stakeholders
2. Track action items derived from terms

## Done
- Record archived
- Agreement shared with parties
- Action items tracked

→ End of Negotiation workflow
