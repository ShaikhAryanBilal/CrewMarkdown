# Negotiation: Agree & Document

## Need
- Parties reached or approaching agreement
- Terms clear enough to document

## Instructions

### 1. Verify Agreement
1. Does the proposed agreement satisfy each party's must-haves?
2. Are there any hidden assumptions or unstated expectations?
3. Is everyone clear on what they're agreeing to?

### 2. Document Terms
1. Write down the agreement terms explicitly
2. Include: what, who, when, and how
3. Note any conditions or contingencies

### 3. Handle Partial Agreement
If full agreement not reached:
1. Document what IS agreed
2. Document remaining disagreements clearly
3. Set a follow-up or escalation path for unresolved items

### 4. Confirm & Sign
1. Each party confirms understanding and acceptance
2. Record sign-off (verbal confirmation is fine, written preferred)
3. Celebrate the agreement — negotiation is hard work

## Done
- Agreement terms documented explicitly
- All parties confirm agreement
- Disagreements (if any) documented with follow-up plan
