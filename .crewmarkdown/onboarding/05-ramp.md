# Onboarding: Ramp to Independence

## Need
- Newcomer has basic competence
- Ready to transition from guided to independent work

## Instructions

### 1. Gradual Ownership Transfer
1. Week 3-4: Newcomer takes ownership of a small feature or module
2. Buddy steps back from active guidance to code-review-only
3. Newcomer starts attending meetings as a contributor, not observer

### 2. Independence Milestones
1. Can pick up tickets from backlog without buddy assignment
2. Can participate in planning with informed estimates
3. Can review others' PRs with useful feedback
4. Can represent their area in cross-team discussions

### 3. Buddy Transition
1. Reduce 1:1s from daily to weekly to bi-weekly
2. Transition primary support to EM/PM
3. Newcomer knows who to ask for each type of question
4. Buddy remains available but initiates less

### 4. Onboarding Completion
1. Review onboarding goals: were they met earlier, on time, or delayed?
2. Celebrate completing onboarding
3. Hand off from buddy to EM for ongoing growth
4. Write a brief handover note: what the newcomer knows, what they're working on, what's next

## Done
- Newcomer owns their work independently
- Buddy relationship transitions to peer relationship
- Onboarding formally completed and celebrated
- Feedback captured for future onboarding improvements

→ End of Onboarding workflow
