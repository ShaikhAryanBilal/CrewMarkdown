# Onboarding: Plan

## Need
- New team member joining
- Onboarding Buddy assigned

## Instructions

### 1. Understand the Newcomer
1. What role are they joining as?
2. What's their background and experience level?
3. What do they already know? (domain, tech stack, tools)
4. What are they most excited to learn?

### 2. Define Onboarding Goals
1. By end of week 1: what should they be able to do?
2. By end of week 2: what should they be comfortable with?
3. By end of month 1: what should they own independently?

### 3. Create Onboarding Plan
1. Week 1: Orientation + Setup + First Task
2. Week 2: Deeper Context + Pairing + First PR
3. Week 3-4: Increasing Independence + Ownership
4. Customize based on role and experience

### 4. Assign Resources
1. People: buddy, mentor, team members to meet
2. Docs: project README, architecture docs, runbooks, style guides
3. Access: repos, tools, environments, credentials (per security policy)
4. Schedule: 1:1s, team meetings, training sessions

## Done
- Newcomer background understood
- Onboarding goals defined per timeframe
- Plan created with weekly milestones
- Resources and access arranged
