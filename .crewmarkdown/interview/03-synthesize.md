# Interview: Synthesize

## Need
- Interview conducted, raw responses captured

## Instructions

### 1. Process Raw Notes
1. Transcribe key parts (if recorded)
2. Organize notes by question or theme
3. Highlight notable quotes (verbatim, attribution)

### 2. Identify Themes & Patterns
1. Read through all responses
2. Look for recurring topics, opinions, concerns, or suggestions
3. Note what's surprising or unexpected

### 3. Extract Insights
1. What did you learn that you didn't know before?
2. What confirms or challenges your assumptions?
3. What's the most actionable finding?

### 4. Write Interview Summary
```markdown
# Interview Summary: [subject/topic]

- **Subject**: [role/description]
- **Interviewer**: [role]
- **Date**: [YYYY-MM-DD]
- **Format**: [structured / semi-structured / unstructured]

## Goals
- [what you wanted to learn]

## Key Insights
1. [insight with supporting quote]
2. [insight with supporting quote]

## Themes
- [theme 1]: [description]
- [theme 2]: [description]

## Notable Quotes
> "[quote]" — [context]

## Actionable Findings
- [finding] → [recommendation]

## Raw Notes
[link to full notes if needed]
```

## Done
- Summary written with insights, themes, and quotes
- Raw notes processed
