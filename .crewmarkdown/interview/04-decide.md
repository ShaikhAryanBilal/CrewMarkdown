# Interview: Decide & Act

## Need
- Interview summary with insights and findings

## Instructions

### 1. Determine Recommendations
1. What should change based on this interview?
2. What's the priority/urgency of each recommendation?
3. Who needs to act on it?

### 2. Assign Action Items
Each action item needs: owner, deliverable, deadline, and source interview

### 3. Share Findings
1. Share summary with relevant stakeholders
2. If multiple interviews — compile cross-cutting findings
3. Update any relevant artifacts (personas, journey maps, PRD, backlog)

## Done
- Recommendations defined
- Action items assigned
- Findings shared with stakeholders
